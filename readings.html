Borges - Garden of Forking Paths

Written in 1941, The Garden of Forking Paths is seen as a prelude to the idea of hypertext and the Internet. How is this idea of the labyrinth and “forking paths” in the story related to new media and the spaces we navigate within the Internet (hypertext)? How does this story allow us to think about different strategies of art making?
